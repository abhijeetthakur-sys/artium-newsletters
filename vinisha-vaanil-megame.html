A Note from Us

We have been watching Vinisha grow as a singer for years now. And we have always known there was a song inside her waiting to come out. This is it.

Vaanil Megame is out today on all streaming platforms. It is a Tamil love song, dreamy and unhurried, the kind that stays with you after it ends. Vinisha recorded it at The Artium Studios, and every listen will tell you she was ready for this moment.

 

Vinisha Vinoth

About Vinisha

Vinisha has been an Artium student for years. Her journey with us has been nothing short of inspiring.

She won the Artium Superstar 2026 competition in the Popular and Film Music (South) category, Under 16. She has performed at unMute Chennai. And through all of that, she has been quietly building toward exactly this moment: her first original for the world to hear.

 

About the Song

The song was written by Divyavalli Santhosh, and the writing is something special. Divyavalli builds the whole song around images: a cloud that touches you, a raindrop from a new moon, the fragrance of flowers, a breeze with nowhere to go. Every line is reaching for something that can barely be named.

 

In the chilling rain, I stood close beside you under your umbrella. Softly, slowly, I breathed in the warmth of your breath. You are my only desire.

The song moves in three layers. The Pallavi opens with sky and cloud and a heart leaping, wide and dreamy. The Anupallavi and Bridge pull inward: unspoken joys, a soul that moves toward someone. And the Charanam brings it all the way down to earth: two people, an umbrella, rain, closeness.

Composed and produced by Akshay Purandare, the arrangement gives Vinisha's voice room to do what it does best: hold the gentleness and carry the longing at the same time.
