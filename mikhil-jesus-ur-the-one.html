Before You Press Play

A Western vocal teacher at Artium. A vocal injury that took him off the stage for months. A song that came out of that.

Jesus You Are The One started as something Mikhil wrote for himself. He composed it, sang it with his voice still recovering, and somewhere in that process it became the most personal thing he has made.

 

The Origin Story

Mikhil’s performance career was going well. Then his voice gave out. A vocal injury took him off the stage entirely. He had to step back from the work he had built.

Most people in that situation just wait to recover and go back to what they were doing. Mikhil wrote a song instead. The lyrics came organically, he says. Honest confessions about loneliness and an existential crisis. About the one constant that had never left. Since childhood, Jesus had been his closest friend, at a time when making real friends was hard. He had felt that presence many times. The song is what it felt like to finally say that in a room with a microphone.

When it was done, he called it “a beautiful testimony to myself and all those who felt lonely in their life pursuits.” He went into the studio, voice not fully healed, and recorded it. He says the grace of God carried the rest.

The song released on 9 June 2026 on all streaming platforms. The music video is out today.

 

Written, composed, and produced by Mikhil George. Music arrangement and production by Albert Wilson. Mixed and mastered by Russel Rodrigues, who also added guitar riffs in the style of John Mayer. The guitar solo Mikhil had always heard in his head was finally played by Nithin Steven.
 

The Song

He wrote this in English, but the grammar is conversational. It reads like a prayer you would say to a friend, not one you would find in a book. The central lyric image is direct: “He knocked at my front door, met me at the porch.” Jesus doesn’t announce himself from a distance. He shows up where you are. For someone who grew up feeling like an outsider and had just lost the stage, that image is the whole song.

The genre is Country Gospel. Guitar-forward, built around a riff and a vocal hook that arrived together: Je-ee-ee-sus. It feels open and unhurried.

Mikhil says the song is for everyone, irrespective of religion, caste, or creed. About belonging and hope at the moment when you feel most alone. “It draws them to the very core of life, that is, the Creator.”

 

“I don’t claim this to be mine but from the hands of God who penned it through me.”

— Mikhil George

About Mikhil

Mikhil George is a Western Vocal teacher at Artium Academy. Based in Kerala, he has been performing and teaching for years. His roots are in Christian devotional music, but his range is wider than that. Country. Gospel. Songs that sit somewhere between faith and storytelling.

This is his first release as an Artium Originals artist. Jesus You Are The One is out now on all streaming platforms and YouTube.

 

From the Music Video Shoot

Shot on the coast of Kerala. The concept was Mikhil’s: American Texas, cowboy, spotlight. The horse was arranged the day before the shoot. Smoke effects came from coconut shells.

Behind Every Artist

None of this happens without the people at home. Through the vocal injury, through the months off the stage, through the long process of finding the right collaborators, Mikhil’s family was there. Quietly. That kind of support makes the difference between stopping and going further.

He says it himself: he would not have had the strength to make this without them.
